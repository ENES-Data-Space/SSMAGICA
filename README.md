# H-Farm Education MAGICA Summer School

In the context of the **H-Farm Education MAGICA Summer School** (Venice, 3-7 July 2023), the lecture **"The role of Data Analytics in the Intenet of Things (IoT)"** will leverage the ENES Data Space, a Jupyter-based data science environment for climate data analysis. More details here: https://enesdataspace.vm.fedcloud.eu

## How to access the ENES Data Space

### Create an EGI Check-in account

The EGI Check-in service provides an easy, fast, and secure way to access the EGI services through your private, institutional, or social media accounts (e.g., Google, Facebook, ...). Click [here](https://aai.egi.eu/signup) to create a new account.

### Join the ENES Virtual Organization

After your account is registered, click [here](https://aai.egi.eu/registry/co_petitions/start/coef:231) to enroll to the ENES Virtual Organization. 

The "BEGIN" button will redirect you to a compilation form. Insert your motivation (e.g., "Participation to the MAGICA Summer School") and agree to the Acceptable Use Policy before submitting your request.

As soon as possible a notification will be sent by email once the request is approved.
